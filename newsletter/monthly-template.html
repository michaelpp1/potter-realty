Seasonal Insights

What to Expect This Time of Year

Spring marks a significant shift in real estate activity. As temperatures warm and families begin planning summer moves, inventory typically increases and buyer competition intensifies. March and April are often when the most desirable homes hit the market — and when they attract the strongest offers.

Curb appeal matters more than ever during spring showings — fresh landscaping, clean exteriors, and well-lit spaces make a significant impression. For buyers, getting pre-approved now means you can move fast when the right home appears.

For You

What to Expect Right Now

For Sellers

  • Spring buyers are motivated — well-priced homes are selling quickly
  • Small updates (fresh paint, staging) significantly increase perceived value
  • Consider a pre-listing inspection to get ahead of negotiation surprises
  • Pricing strategy is critical — overpricing leads to longer days on market
  

For Buyers

  • Get pre-approved before searching — competition moves fast in spring
  • Be ready to act — top homes can receive multiple offers within days
  • Define your must-haves vs. nice-to-haves to make faster decisions
  • Don't overlook slightly overpriced listings — they may have room to negotiate
